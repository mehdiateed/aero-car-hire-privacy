1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you register and use our app, we collect:

  • Account Information: Name, email, phone number, password
  • Address Information: Pickup and drop-off addresses you enter for ride bookings
  • Booking Information: Trip history, service preferences, destinations
  • Payment Information: Processed securely via Razorpay (we don't store card details)
  • Driver Information: License details, vehicle information, insurance documents
  • Device Information: Device type, OS version, app version

Address Information

We collect address information entered by you to:

  • Process ride bookings and service requests
  • Assign appropriate drivers through our admin team
  • Calculate estimated fares based on route distance
  • Maintain booking records for service history and quality

Note: We do not track real-time GPS location during trips. Location data is limited to the pickup and drop-off addresses you provide.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Delivery: Process bookings, assign drivers to customer requests through our admin team, calculate fares
  • Communication: Send booking confirmations, trip updates, and receipts
  • Safety: Verify driver credentials, monitor for fraud, resolve disputes
  • Improvement: Analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, develop new features
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with laws and respond to legal requests

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only when necessary:

  • With Other Users: Customers see driver details during trips; drivers see customer pickup addresses
  • Service Providers:
    • Supabase (database and authentication)
    • Razorpay (payment processing)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect rights and safety

4. Data Security

We protect your information using:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication and encrypted passwords
  • PCI-DSS compliant payment processing
  • Code obfuscation and regular security updates
  • Access controls and staff training

Note: No internet transmission is 100% secure. We strive to protect your data but cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data retained while account is active
  • Trip History: Retained for 7 years (legal and tax requirements)
  • Address Data: Retained for 1 year for dispute resolution and service improvement
  • Deleted Accounts: Most data deleted within 30 days

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View and download your personal data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information in app settings
  • Deletion: Delete your account and request data removal
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails and disable notifications
  • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format
